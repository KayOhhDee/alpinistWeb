Our Packages

Survival Kit

We provide our alpinist with survival kit once they book a mountaineering adventure with us. The Kit include tent and other stuff that will enable you to start fire and have safety.

Guided Trips

Our mountaineering trips are guided by our experts who will train you to become an outstanding mountaineer. They will show you all the secrets there is to becoming a great alpinist.

Wi-Fi Spots

We have local wi-fi spots where you can enjoy high speed internet as you embark on our trips. No need to worry about internet as you spend days or weeks with us ascending mountains.

Racing Challenge

Ready for a competition? You can sign up for our mountain marathons. Attempt to reach the mountain peak before anyone else and win the trophy and other prizes.
